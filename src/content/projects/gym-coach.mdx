## What I'm building

A workout coach I'll actually use — not another tracker that gets abandoned in
a week. The goal is an app that plans sessions, logs lifts quickly, and adapts
programming based on real performance (progressive overload, deloads, missed
sessions).

This is **in progress** — it's part of a broader push to build tools that make
my own systems feel seamless.

## Planned scope

- Fast workout logging (sets, reps, weight) with minimal taps.
- Progress tracking and trend charts per exercise.
- Adaptive programming: suggest next session's targets from recent history.
- (Stretch) integration with Apple Health / wearables for a seamless data loop.

> case study as the app takes shape.
